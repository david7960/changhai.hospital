PORTLARS Calculator V1.0

CATS (Chronic Pancreatitis CT Severity Model) is an automatic, economical, and user-friendly prediction model that accurately predicts the severity of chronic pancreatitis (CP). It reflects the morphological features of the pancreas and predicts exocrine function, as well as the short- and medium-term prognosis for patients with CP.

The interactive CATS is easy to use. Simply input the five CT characteristics shown below, and the severity of CP will be presented.

CATS does not collect or store any individual data.
